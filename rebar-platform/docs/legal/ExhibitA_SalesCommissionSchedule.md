# Exhibit A: Sales Commission Schedule (Template)

*This document is a non-binding template and does not constitute legal advice. Engage qualified counsel before use.*

## Commission Logic
- AI-closed deals: 0% rep, 10% marketing fund.
- AI-sourced, human-closed: 5% rep, 5% marketing fund.
- Human-only deals: standard rep commission to be defined per territory.

## Payment Terms
- Calculated monthly based on recognized net revenue.
- Adjustments for refunds or clawbacks reflected in the next period.
