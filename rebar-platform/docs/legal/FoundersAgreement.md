# Founders Agreement (Template)

*This document is a non-binding template and does not constitute legal advice. Engage qualified counsel before use.*

## Parties
- Founders of the rebar platform venture.

## Key Terms
- **Equity splits**: allocate founder equity with standard four-year vesting and one-year cliff.
- **Roles**: product/engineering lead, operations lead, go-to-market lead.
- **IP assignment**: all platform IP assigned to the company.
- **Decision-making**: unanimous for foundational items; majority for day-to-day.
- **Confidentiality**: protect customer data, fabrication processes, and pricing.
